    About me

    I have a broad work background ranging from sweeping floors to technical positions in the OilField. These positions have made me look for something different. I chose to become a developer as it offers a chance to be learning in your everyday work, and also a great opportunity to be a part of a team striving to reach goals.

    I was able to see how being a developer can be through my wife who is currently a developer. The environment and work life balance showed me what being a developer can be like in that sense. Her passion for writing code intrigued me, I personally didn't understand the appeal of solving a coding problem until I started coding myself. It is satisfying when you hit the “AH-HA”moment of the problem that you have been trying to solve.

    I find web development to be one big puzzle that is challenging and keeps my brain constantly working and learning. I love to learn new things and be proficient at them. Web development provides an avenue onto which I can always be learning something new and not feel stagnant in my job.

    While working as a shipping clerk I worked with an outdated inventory program. The whole time I worked with this program I couldn't help thinking there has to be a more efficient way of doing this, with not so many errors. With this thought in mind Im glad I decided to make a career change into being a developer.
